Key Takeaways

Emission Reductions

LNG conversions achieve up to 90% emission reductions compared to diesel engines, with BioLNG providing even greater environmental benefits as a renewable fuel source. Heavy-duty applications see the most significant improvements.

Cost Efficiency

Dual-fuel systems offer 20% diesel savings while maintaining operational flexibility. Dedicated LNG systems provide maximum fuel cost reductions, particularly beneficial for long-haul operations with predictable routes.

Performance Options

Multiple conversion approaches available: fumigation systems for quick upgrades, sequential injection for precision control, and complete engine replacement for maximum performance and compliance with Euro 4-6 standards.
